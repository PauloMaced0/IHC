Synopsis
"Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind" is a book by Yuval Noah Harari that spans the history of human evolution from the emergence of archaic human species in the Stone Age up to the twenty-first century, focusing on Homo sapiens. The genre of "Sapiens" blends history with science and anthropology, providing insights into how humans have come to dominate the planet.
